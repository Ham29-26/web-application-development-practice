London

London is the capital and largest city of both England and the United Kingdom, renowned as a global hub for finance, culture, and history.

Situated on the River Thames, it is a diverse,, bustling metropolis of ~9 million people known for iconic landmarks like the Tower of London, museums, and vibrant theater scenes.

Paris

Paris, the capital of France and nicknamed the "City of Light," is a global hub for art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture located along the Seine River.

Famous for iconic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and Louvre Museum, it offers a historic atmosphere with 20 distinct arrondissements, drawing millions of tourists annually.
